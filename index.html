Move Set

As you may already guessed, Mona is a Hydro Catalyst user

Normal Attack: Ripple of Fate

Normal Attack: Perform up to 4 water splash attacks that deal Hydro DMG.
Charged Attack: Consumes a bit of stamina and deals AOE Hydro DMG after a short casting period.
Plunging Attack: Gathering the might of Hydro, Mona plunges towards the ground from mid-air, damaging all opponents in her path. Deals AOE Hydro DMG upon impact with the ground.

Elemental Skill: Mirror Reflection of Doom

Creates an illusory Phantom of fate from coalesced waterspouts.
The Phantom has the following special properties:
  • Continuously taunts nearby opponents, attracting their fire.
  • Each second, 4 times, deals AOE Hydro DMG.
  • When its duration expires, the Phantom explodes, dealing AOE Hydro DMG.

    • Elemental Burst: Stellaris Phantasm

    Mona summons the sparkling waves and creates a reflection of the starry sky, applying the Illusory Bubble status to the opponents in a large AoE.
    Illusionary Bubble: Traps opponents inside a pocket of destiny and also makes them wet. Renders weaker opponents immobile. When an opponent affected by Illusory Bubble sustains DMG, it has the following effects:
  • Applies an Omen to the opponent, which gives a DMG Bonus, also increasing the DMG of the attack that causes it.
  • Removes the Illusory Bubble, dealing Hydro DMG in the process.
    • Omen: During its duration, increases DMG taken by opponents.

    Artifacts

    Recommended Artifacts for Mona

    There are two ways you can build Mona: Burst Support or Main DPS

    Burst Support

    Burst Support comes in handy for crowd control as Mona's burst can render smaller enemies immobile.
    A 2 piece Nobless will increase burst damage by 20% while a 2 piece Depth will increase Mona's Hydro damage by 15%

    Noblesse Oblige x2

    Elemental Burst Damage +20%

    Heart of Depth x2

    Hydro Damage Bonus +15%

    Main DPS

    Other people may say, "Mona is better as a support!" I disagree.
    A 2 piece Gladiator will increase attack damage by 18% while a 2 piece Depth will increase Mona's Hydro damage by 15%.

    Noblesse Oblige x2

    Attack Damge +18%

    Heart of Depth x2

    Hydro Damage Bonus +15%

    Additonally, aim for a Hydro Damage bonus cup for your 5th artifact

    As for substats, aim for ATK, ATK%, Crit%, Crit Rate, or Energy Recharge

    Team Composition

    Who you can pair Mona with

    Honestly, any Electro, Cyro, or Pyro unit works well with Mona

    Based on personal use and research (I say this becuase I don't have Bennett), these 3 characters have pretty good team synergy

    Ganyu

    Freeze

    All Mona's attacks apply a wet status to the enemy. I use Ganyu's Elemental Skill to quickly freeze them after placing down Mona's Elemental Skill.

    Fischl

    Electro-Charge

    Electro-Charge does damage and stuns enemies. Drop own Oz and switch back to Mona to apply wet to deal electro-charged reactions.

    Bennett

    Vapourize

    Bennett is an S-tier support character. Bennett's Burst allows a damage boost in an AOE and when a combo is done correctly, Mona's burst can do VERY big damage.

    Damage Calculator

    You've built Mona, and want to know how much damage she can do?

    Here's a code example for a very simple Damage Calculator!

            #SCENARIO: YOU ARE FIGHTING A LEVEL 87 HILICHURL
        #WITH MY FULLY BUILT LEVEL 90 C0 MONA WITH
        #A LEVEL 90 EYE OF PERCEPTION

        #MONA ATTACK
        charATK = 287                                       #Mona's base attack
        weaponATK = 454                                     #Weapon's base attack
        weaponBonus = 1 + (55.1/100)                        #Eye of Percepetion DMG substat
        baseATK = charATK + weaponATK

        #ARTIFACTS
        artiATK = 1706                                      #How much extra damage from artifacts (the green number in attributes)
        attack = baseATK + artiATK

        #BONUSES
        abilityPercent = 60.2/100                           #First auto Attack. Found under the Talents
        bonusDMG = 88/100                                   #Hydro DMG Bonus

        #ENEMY HILICHURL DEFENSE
        monaLVL = 90
        hiliLVL = 87
        hiliRES = 1 - 0.1

        mulDEF = (monaLVL + 100) / (monaLVL + hiliLVL + 200)

        outDMG = attack * abilityPercent * (1 + bonusDMG)
        inDMG = outDMG * mulDEF * hiliRES
        crit = inDMG * (1 + 99.7/100)

        #APPROX DAMAGE DEALT TO THE HILICHURL
        print(round(inDMG))
        print(round(crit))

    There may be a +/- of 2 to the results
